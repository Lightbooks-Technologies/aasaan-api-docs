## Jivochat

*Integrate JivoChat with Your Store*  
*Set up JivoChat on your Store*

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*  
Jivochat is an easy-to-use chat software which you can add on your website for customers to chat with your support team. With aasaan, you can easily set up Jivochat on your store and display your chat button for customers. The steps are as follows:

*Note: Make sure to install JivoChat from the app market. Click [here](#) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**

1. Login to your Jivochat account and click on the Manage button shown below.

**STEP 2:**

2. In your connected channels, click on your website.

**STEP 3:**

3. Click on Installation.

**STEP 4:**

4. Copy the widget ID from the code shown below.

**STEP 5:**

5. Go to your installed Jivochat app in aasaan. Enter the widget ID and click on Verify.
   Your Jivochat widget should be successfully installed.
