## Tidio Live Chat

*Integrate Tidio Live Chat with Your Store*  
*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*  
You can watch the video version of the article [here](#).

Tidio offers live chat tools that help your business provide better customer support and get more sales. With aasaan, you can easily set up Tidio chat on your store. The steps to do so are as follows:

*Note: Make sure to install Tidio Live Chat from the app market. Click [here](#) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**

1. Login to your Tidio account and click on Settings.

**STEP 2:**

2. Click on Installation.

**STEP 3:**

3. Copy the live chat API key shown which lies between "code.tidio.co/" and ".js".

**STEP 4:**

4. Go to your installed Tidio app in aasaan. Enter the API key from step 4 and click on Verify.
   The Tidio chat widget is successfully installed on your website.
