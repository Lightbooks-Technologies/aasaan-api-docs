## LiveChat

*Integrate LiveChat with Your Store*  
*Set up LiveChat on your Store*

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*  
You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

LiveChat provides Chatbot and helpdesk solutions for businesses so that they can manage customer interactions and boost satisfaction. You can easily set up LiveChat on your aasaan store. The steps for doing so are as follows:

*Note: Make sure to install LiveChat from the app market. Click [here](#) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**

1. Login to your LiveChat account and click on Settings.

**STEP 2:**

2. Click on Install chat widget manually.

**STEP 3:**

3. Click on Copy code.

**STEP 4:**

4. Your Livechat widget code will look like this. We need to only copy the license ID as highlighted below.

**STEP 5:**

5. Go to your installed LiveChat app in aasaan. Enter the License ID and click on Verify.
   LiveChat should be successfully installed on your website.
