## Richpanel Live Chat

*Integrate Richpanel Live Chat with Your Store*  
*Set up Richpanel Chat on your Store*

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*  
You can watch the video version of the article [here](#).

Richpanel helps you provide effortless Customer Service for Ecommerce brands. You can automatically resolve common questions, eliminate manual tasks, and save your time with a CRM built for ecommerce. You can easily integrate Richpanel with your aasaan store. The steps are as follows:

*Note: Make sure to install Richpanel from the aasaan app market. Click [here](#) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**

1. Login to your Richpanel account and click on Setup Customer Widget.

**STEP 2:**

2. Click on Install widget.

**STEP 3:**

3. Click on Use code snippet.

**STEP 4:**

4. Enter your website URL.

**STEP 5:**

5. Your API key will appear. Click on the Copy icon to copy the key.

**STEP 6:**

6. Go to your installed Richpanel app in aasaan. Enter the API key from step 5 and click on Verify.
   You have successfully added Richpanel chat to your website.
