## Zoho Chat

*Integrate Zoho Chat with Your Store*  
*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*  
You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

Zoho chat is an effective customer support tool that enables businesses to offer amazing support at all stages of the customer lifecycle, boosting conversions and revenue. You can easily add Zoho chat to your aasaan store. The steps to do so are as follows:

*Note: Make sure to install Zoho Chat from the app market. Click [here](#) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**

1. Login to your Zoho Chat account (you can access it at [https://desk.zoho.in](https://desk.zoho.in)) and click on the Settings button shown.

**STEP 2:**

2. Click on Chat.

**STEP 3:**

3. Copy the widget code highlighted as shown below.

**STEP 4:**

4. Go to your installed Zoho Chat app in aasaan. Enter the Zoho Chat widget code from step 3 and click on Verify.
   Your Zoho chat has been successfully set up.
