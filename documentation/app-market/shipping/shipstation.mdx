## Shipstation

*How to Set up Shipstation on your Store*  
*Integrate Shipstation to your Store*

*Note: Click [here](mobile-admin-link) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

Shipstation is one of the most renowned global shipping aggregators that helps over 130,000 businesses ship with over 100 courier providers. You can easily integrate Shipstation with your aasaan store to streamline your order fulfillment process. The steps for setting up Shipstation are as follows:

*Note: Make sure to install Shipstation from the app market. Click [here](install-app-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**

1. Login to your Shipstation account and click on My Profile.

**STEP 2:**

2. Click on API Settings.

**STEP 3:**

3. Copy the given API keys.

**STEP 4:**

4. Go to your installed Shipstation app in aasaan. Enter the API keys from step 3 and click on Verify.
   You have successfully set up Shipstation on your store.
