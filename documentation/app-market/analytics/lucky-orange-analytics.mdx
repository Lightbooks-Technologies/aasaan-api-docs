## Lucky Orange Analytics

**Set up Lucky Orange Analytics on Your Website**  
*Connect Lucky Orange to your Store*  
*Note: Click [here](mobile-admin-link) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

Lucky Orange is an analytics platform with which to understand the behavior of your website visitors. You can view user recordings, heatmaps that show where your visitors are clicking, and can run surveys to get visitor feedback.  
If you have a Lucky Orange account that you wish to set up on your aasaan website to track user behavior, the steps to do so are as follows:  
*Note: Make sure to install Lucky Orange from the app market. Click [here](app-market-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

### STEP 1:

Login to your Lucky Orange account and click on Settings.

### STEP 2:

Click on Sites.

### STEP 3:

Copy the given site ID for your website.

### STEP 4:

Go to your Lucky Orange app in aasaan. Enter the site ID and click on Verify.  
You have successfully enabled Lucky Orange on your website.
