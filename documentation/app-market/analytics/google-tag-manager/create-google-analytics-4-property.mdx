## Create Google Analytics 4 Property

**How to Create your Google Analytics 4 Property**

**STEP 1:**
Login to Google Analytics and click on Admin.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Create Property.

**STEP 3:**
Enter the Property name, country, currency and click on Next.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Select one to choose your industry category.

**STEP 5:**
Select your category. Here we will select Shopping.

**STEP 6:**
Choose your Business size and click on Next.

**STEP 7:**
Choose your relevant business objectives.

**STEP 8:**
Click on Create to create the property.

**STEP 9:**
Now we need to create the data stream to capture user activity on your website. To do that, click on Web.

**STEP 10:**
Enter your website URL, stream name and click on Create Stream.

**STEP 11:**
Your stream has been successfully created. Use the measurement ID shown use it to verify your Google Tag Manager integration in aasaan.

**STEP 12:**
Click on Next to proceed further.

**STEP 13:**
Click on Continue to Home to visit your property dashboard.
