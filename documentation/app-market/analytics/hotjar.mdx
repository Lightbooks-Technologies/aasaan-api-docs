## Hotjar

**Set up Hotjar on Your Website**  
*Connect Hotjar to your Store*  
*Note: Click [here](mobile-admin-link) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

Hotjar is a highly effective tool you can use to understand how visitors behave on your website. You can view user recordings, heatmaps that show where your visitors are clicking, and can run surveys to get visitor feedback. If you have an existing Hotjar account that you wish to set up on your aasaan website to track detailed analytics, here's how you do it:  
*Note: Make sure to install Hotjar from the app market. Click [here](app-market-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

### STEP 1:

Login to your Hotjar Workspace you wish to link to your website.

### STEP 2:

Click on the profile button in the top right corner and go to settings.

### STEP 3:

Copy the Hotjar tracking ID shown.

### STEP 4:

Go to your Hotjar app in aasaan. Paste the tracking ID and click on Verify.  
You have successfully added the Hotjar tracking code to your website.
