## Create your Meta Pixel

**How to Create your Meta Pixel**

**STEP 1:**
Login to your Meta business account, go to the business settings page and click on the All Tools button shown.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Events Manager.

**STEP 3:**
Click on Connect Data Sources.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Web.

**STEP 5:**
Enter the pixel name and click on Create Pixel.

**STEP 6:**
Enter your website URL and click on Check.

**STEP 7:**
Click on Next.

**STEP 8:**
Select Meta Pixel only and click on Next. Once that is done, refresh the page.

**STEP 9:**
Your Meta Pixel has been successfully created. You can paste the Pixel ID shown in aasaan's Meta Pixel integration to add the Pixel to your store.
