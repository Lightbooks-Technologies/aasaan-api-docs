## Meta Standard Events

**What events can your Meta Pixel track on your website?**

**Events Tracked by Facebook**

The main purpose of setting up a Meta Pixel on your website is to track certain actions taken by users, which can be used to optimize your Facebook ad campaigns further.

But what events can you send from your aasaan website? They are as follows:

**Website Action** | **Description**
--- | ---
Page view | Each time a page loads or the browser history state is changed by the active site. This event is tracked by default.
Add to Cart | The addition of an item to a shopping cart or basket. For example, clicking an Add to Cart button on a website.
Initiate Checkout | The start of a checkout process. For example, clicking a Checkout button.
Purchase | The completion of a purchase, usually signified by receiving order or purchase confirmation, or a transaction receipt. For example, landing on a Thank You or confirmation page.

**How to View your Pixel Events**

You can follow the given steps to view your Meta Pixel events in the events manager.

**STEP 1:**
Login to your Meta business account and click on the All Tools button in your business settings as shown.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Events Manager.

**STEP 3:**
Click on the Pixel whose events you wish to track.

**STEP 4:**
You can track all your events in the overview page shown below. The date-wise event count is shown in the overview.
You can alter the date range by clicking on the calendar icon.

**STEP 5:**
Upon scrolling down, you will be able to see the list of events picked up by your Pixel.

**STEP 6:**
You can click on an event to track its date-wise frequency.
