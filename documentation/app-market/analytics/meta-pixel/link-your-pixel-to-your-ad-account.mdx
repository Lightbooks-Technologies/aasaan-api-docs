## Link your Pixel to your Ad Account

**How to Link your Meta Pixel to your Ad Account**

This article will show you how to link your Facebook pixel to your ad account so that you can remarket appropriately to your audience.

**STEP 1:**
Go to the business settings of your Facebook account. Click on Data Sources > Pixels.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Add Assets.

**STEP 3:**
Select the Ad account you wish to add. Then click on Add.
Your ad account and pixel have been linked. Click on Done.
