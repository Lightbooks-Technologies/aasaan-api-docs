## Shipping

Before you receive your first order, you need to have a shipping strategy. You can either ship your orders on your own or partner with one or more shipping carriers.

aasaan offers easy integration with these shipping carriers:
- Pickrr
- Shiprocket
- iThink Logistics
- Delhivery
- Borzo
- Shipstation (for merchants in North America)

In this section, you will learn how to integrate the carriers mentioned above into your store.
