## Ecomail Integration Guide

**Integrate Ecomail with Your Store**  
*Note: Click [here](mobile-admin-link) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*

Ecomail is a comprehensive marketing platform that facilitates effective communication with your customers through various channels such as Email, SMS, and social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Messenger. Integrating Ecomail with your store enhances your marketing strategies and customer engagement.

*Important:*
- Ensure Ecomail is installed from the aasaan app market. For installation guidance, click [here](install-app-link).

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Integration Steps">

  ### Integrating Ecomail with Your Store

  Follow these steps to integrate Ecomail with your aasaan store, allowing you to leverage its marketing tools for your business.

**STEP 1:**
Login to your Ecomail dashboard, click on your profile icon at the top right corner, and then click on Domains.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Integration.

**STEP 3:**
Copy the API key shown.

**STEP 4:**
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and install Ecomail from your app market. Click [here](install-app-link) to learn how to install apps from the app market.

**STEP 5:**
Paste the API key you got from step 3.

**STEP 6:**
Click on Verify.

You have successfully set up your Ecomail integration.
