## Shopping

With aasaan, you have the ability to sell your products online on different sales channels, such as Google Merchant Center and Facebook Shop. This has the benefit of presenting your products to a wider audience, which leads to greater traffic and sales.

In this section, you will learn how to connect two of the most popular online sales channels - Google Merchant Center (Google Shopping) and Facebook Shop - to your aasaan store.
