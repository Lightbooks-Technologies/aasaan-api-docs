Privy is a very handy email marketing tool which you can use to build your list of subscribers for your business. With aasaan's privy integration, you can easily display popups on your website to build your email list. Here's how you do it.

*Note: Make sure to install Privy from the app market. Click [here](install-app-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**  
Login to your Privy dashboard and click on your business name in the top right corner. A drop-down will open, wherein you have to click on Business Information.

**STEP 2:**  
Click on Privy Code.

**STEP 3:**  
Copy the account identifier shown below.

**STEP 4:**  
Go to your installed Privy app in aasaan. Paste the account identifier from step 3 in the field shown and click on Verify.  
You have successfully set up your Privy popup on your website.
