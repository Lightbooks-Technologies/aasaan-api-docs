## Nudgify

*Note: Click [here](mobile-admin-link) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

Nudgify is an easy-to-use, effective tool for displaying social proof on your website. With it, you can display notifications such as "X customers have purchased this product" or "X users have visited this website". This is highly effective for building credibility with prospective customers.

Here's how you set up Nudgify on your website:

**STEP 1:**
Login to your Nudgify account and click on Install my Pixel.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Add to your HTML.

**STEP 3:**
Copy the uuid code as shown below.

**STEP 4:**
Install Nudgify from the app market. Click [here](install-link) to learn how.

**STEP 5:**
Paste the uuid code copied from step 3 and click on Verify.
You have added your Nudgify widget on your website.
