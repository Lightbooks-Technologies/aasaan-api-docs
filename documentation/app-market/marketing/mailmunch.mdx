## Mailmunch

*Note: Click [here](mobile-admin-link) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

Mailmunch is a marketing platform that helps you collect leads and provide your users a more engaging experience with your brand. With it, you can grab the attention of your website visitors with rich forms and popups. You can use their tools to build an email list and engage your leads with automated emails and broadcast campaigns to get more sales.

Here's how you easily set up Mailmunch on your aasaan website.

**Note:** Make sure to install Mailmunch from the aasaan app market. Click [here](install-app-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.

**STEP 1:**
Login to your Mailmunch dashboard and click on Site Settings.

**STEP 2:**
Click on GET SITE CODE.

**STEP 3:**
Copy the site ID from the code shown below.

**STEP 4:**
Go to your installed Mailmunch app in aasaan. Paste your site Id from step 3 and click on Verify. Your Mailmunch popup will display the status "Active".

**STEP 5:**
Return to your Mailmunch dashboard and click on Verify Code to confirm publishing your popup.

You have successfully added your Mailmunch popup to your website.
