Wisepop is an onsite marketing platform that helps you convert website visitors into customers. From spin-to-win to lead capture forms, to countdown offers, you can create beautiful & meaningful messages your visitors enjoy engaging with.

*Note: Make sure to install Wisepops from the app market. Click [here](install-app-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**  
Login to your Wisepops dashboard and click on Settings.

**STEP 2:**  
Click on Install Wisepops.  
Copy the highlighted part of the code, as shown below.

**STEP 3:**  
Go to your installed Wisepops app in aasaan. Paste the code snippet you got from step 2 and click on Verify.  
Your Wisepops popup campaign will be successfully set up.
