Zotabox is a very handy popup marketing tool which you can use to get leads for your store. Furthermore, you get access to 20+ marketing tools with which you can take your marketing to the next level. You can easily integrate Zotabox to your store. Here's how you do it.

*Note: Make sure to install Zotabox from the app market. Click [here](install-app-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**  
Login to your Zotabox dashboard and click on Your Embed Code.

**STEP 2:**  
Copy the code snippet between .com/ and /widgets.js. For example, here the snippet we would be copying is 2/f/2f835d4e40a978f5b2c569059b021158. We have highlighted it in the screenshot below.

**STEP 3:**  
Go to your installed Zotabox app in aasaan. Paste the code snippet you copied from step 2 and click on Verify.  
You have successfully set up Zotabox on your store.
