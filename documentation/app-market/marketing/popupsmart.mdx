Popupsmart is a no-code tool you can add to your website in order to increase e-commerce sales, build email lists, and engage with your visitors.

**Here's how you add it to your website with aasaan:**

**STEP 1:**  
Login to your Popupsmart dashboard and click on Verify Now.  
*Note: If the announcement bar shown is not visible on your dashboard, then click on Account > Your embed code.*

**STEP 2:**  
Copy the part of the code highlighted below.

**STEP 3:**  
Install Popupsmart from the app market. Click [here](install-app-link) to learn how to install apps from your app market.

**STEP 4:**  
Paste the part of the code highlighted in step 2.  
Click on Verify.  
You have successfully installed your Popupsmart code on your website.

**STEP 5:**  
Go back to your Popupsmart dashboard and click on Next Step.

**STEP 6:**  
Click on Verify your site to verify that your Popupsmart code has been installed on your site.
