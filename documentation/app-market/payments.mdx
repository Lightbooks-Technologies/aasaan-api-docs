---
title: "Payments"
---

If you wish to accept online payments on your store, **aasaan** has multiple payment gateways that you can integrate with your store.

You can integrate any one of these payment gateways:   
    - aasaan Pay
    - Razorpay
    - PayU
    - Stripe
    - Paypal

In this section, you will learn how to set up these payment gateways on your store so that you can start receiving online payments.
