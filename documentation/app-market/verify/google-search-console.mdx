## Google Search Console

*Verify your Website on Google Search Console*  
*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*  

The first step to getting your website noticed on Google is to verify your ownership. You can do this via Google Search Console.

*Note: Make sure to install the Google Search Console app from the aasaan app market. Click [here](#) to learn how to install apps from your app market.*

**STEP 1:**

1. Create a Google Search Console account.

**STEP 2:**

2. Enter your website URL in the format below (https://yourwebsite) in the URL prefix section. Click on Continue.

**STEP 3:**

3. Expand the row titled "HTML tag" and copy the code snippet given. You need to copy the part of the tag within the quotation marks that comes after content=. For example, if the entire HTML tag is `<meta content="samplecodesnippet" name="google-site-verification"/>`, then you need to copy just `samplecodesnippet` for configuring the Search Console app in aasaan.

**STEP 4:**

4. To paste that snippet log in to your Aasaan desktop admin panel and install Google Search Console from your app market. Click [here](#) to learn how to install apps.

**STEP 5:**

5. Go to your installed Google Search Console app in aasaan. Enter the part of the meta tag snippet from step 3 and click on Verify.

**STEP 4:**

6. Choose the Google account with which you created your search console account.

**STEP 5:**

7. Click on Continue. Your code snippet has successfully been added.

**STEP 6:**

8. Go back to your Google Search Console and click on Verify. Your website is now verified.
