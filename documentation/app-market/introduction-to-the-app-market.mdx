title: "Introduction to the App Market"
---

In **Aasaan** app market, you can find several apps to help you manage every aspect of your store. We offer apps for payments, shipping, adding sales channels, marketing, analytics, and much more.

### How to Access your App Market

Login to your Aasaan desktop admin panel and click on **_App Market_**.
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_1.png" />
               </Frame>

Here you can see all the apps available.
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_2.png" />
               </Frame>

Click on the **_Filter by_** dropdown to view and filter the app categories.
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_3.png" />
               </Frame>

### How to Install Apps

Click on the app you wish to install. For example, here we will be installing **_Bing Webmaster_**.
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_4.png" />
               </Frame>

Click on the **_Install_** button.
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_5.png" />
               </Frame>

Your app will be installed, and you will be taken to the page to enter the required credentials to integrate your app. Here we need to add the Bing Webmaster API key.
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_6.png" />
               </Frame>

### How to View Installed Apps

You can view installed apps by going to **_App Market > My Apps_**.
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_7.png" />
               </Frame>

### Points to Remember
<Tip>When you install an app without completely integrating it, its status is **_Configure_**.</Tip>
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_8.png" />
               </Frame>
<br/>
<Tip>After the integration, its status changes to **_Active_**.</Tip>
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_9.png" />
               </Frame>
<br/>
<Tip>You can deactivate an installed app by clicking on the "⋮" button and then clicking on **_Disable_**.</Tip>
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_10.png" />
               </Frame>
<br/>
<Tip>Once deactivated, the status will say **_Inactive_**.</Tip>
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_11.png" />
               </Frame>
<br/>
<Tip>If you wish to reactivate an app, click on the "⋮" button and click on **_Enable_**.</Tip>
<Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/introduction/introduction_12.png" />
               </Frame>
