## Brands

### Add Brands to Your Store
#### How to create Brands in your Store

*Note: [Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article](#)*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

One way to organize your products on your store is by categories. But did you know you could also organize them by brands? Here is how you add brands to your store.

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Brands.

**STEP 2:**
Click on +New.

**STEP 3:**
Upload your brand logo, and enter the name and description.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Save.

Your brand has been created.
