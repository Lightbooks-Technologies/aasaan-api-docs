One way to organize your products on your store is by categories. But did you know you could also organize them by brands? Here is how you add brands to your store.

### How to Access Brands

Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on **_Brands_**.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/brands/brands_1.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Add a Brand

Click on **_+New_**.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/brands/brands_2.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Brand Details

Upload your brand logo, and enter the name and description. 
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/brands/brands_3.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Save Brand

Click on Save.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/brands/brands_4.png" />
                    </Frame>

Your brand has been created.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/brands/brands_5.png" />
                    </Frame>
