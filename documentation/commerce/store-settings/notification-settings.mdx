## Notification Settings

*How to Manage your Store notifications settings*

[Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article](#)

With aasaan, you can decide whether you wish to receive push notifications, and what kinds of notifications you would like to receive. This can be done in your notifications settings. The steps to configure your notifications settings are as follows:

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan admin panel and click on Manage.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Notification Settings.

**STEP 3:**
Enable the toggle for Allow Notifications to enable all push notifications for your store.
You have enabled all notifications.

**STEP 4:**
In the same way, enable the toggle for Stores to enable notifications for your orders.

**STEP 5:**
Finally, enable the toggle for Visits if you wish to be notified about store visits.
You have successfully configured your store notification settings.
