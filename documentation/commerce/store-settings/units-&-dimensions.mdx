## Units & Dimensions

*How to Setup your Standard Units and Dimensions*
*How to Set your Units & Dimensions*

[Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article](#)

[You can watch the video version of this article here](#)

Here's how you can set your desired units and dimensions in aasaan.

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Manage.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Units and Dimensions.

**STEP 3:**
Click on the weight unit drop down to set the desired unit for your product weights.
Select your desired weight unit. Here we will select kilograms.

**STEP 5:**
Click on the dimensions unit drop down to set the desired unit for your product dimensions. Here we will select centimeters.

**STEP 6:**
Click on Save to save your settings.

**STEP 8:**
Click on Okay to confirm your changes.
Your settings have been successfully saved.
To verify this, you can go to any product to view the units you've set.
