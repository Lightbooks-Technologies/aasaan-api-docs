## Tax Fields

*How to add tax fields for your products*
*How to add your taxation information*

[You can watch the video version of this article here](#)

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan admin panel and click on Manage.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Tax Fields.

**STEP 3:**
Click on Create Tax Field.

**STEP 4:**
Enter the tax name, tax label, and tax value (the tax rate).
*Notes:*
1. The tax label will be visible to the customer during the checkout process and in the order receipt.
2. The tax value you choose here will apply to all the products in your store. If you wish to assign different tax rates to different products, you can do it in the Product level taxes section when you create/edit a product.
Choose the channel in which your tax rate will apply.
*Note: The feature of choosing a channel for the tax rate is only offered on the business plan.*

**STEP 5:**
Mark the tax field as "Enabled" and click on Save.

**STEP 6:**
Your tax field has been successfully saved.
