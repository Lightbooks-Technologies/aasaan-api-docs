## Customers

In this article, you will learn how to view your customer details.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="Steps">
        To view the customer data on your Aasaan store.:
        - Log in to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on **Customers**.
        - Here you will see your entire list of customers. You will see the customer's mobile number and/or email address, and also the date of their first order under the Joined column. Click on a customer name to view further details about them.
        - At the top, the customer's contact details will be displayed.
        - You can view the customer's address(es) on the left-hand side as shown.
        - You can also view the orders they have placed on your store. You can click on an order to go to that order in the orders screen.
    </Tab>
