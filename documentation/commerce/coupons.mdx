On your aasaan store, you can create discount coupons for special sales like festivals and end of season sales.   

<Note>These coupons will apply to your entire store. Here is how you do it.</Note>

### How to Access Coupons

Log in to your aasaan desktop admin panel, and click on **_Coupons_**.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_1.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Add a Coupon

Click on the button **_+New_**.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_2.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Coupon Details

Enter the **_coupon name_** and the **_discount code_** your customers will have to enter.   
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_3.png" />
                    </Frame>

You can also enter a **_coupon description_**. 
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_4.png" />
                    </Frame>
        <br />
        <Note>Note: Discount codes are case-sensitive. For example, DUMMY1 and Dummy1 will be considered different discount codes.</Note>

### Coupon Visibility

Coupons are displayed on the product page by default. If you do not wish to display your coupon, you can enable the **_Hide coupon on the website?_** toggle. 
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_5.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Coupon Type

Choose your **_coupon type_**. In this guide, we will click on Discount and offer a fixed price discount coupon. 
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_6.png" />
                    </Frame>
        <br />
        <Note>Note: The steps that follow are the same even if you choose to create a percentage-based Discount.</Note>

### Channel

Choose the **_channel_** for which you wish your coupon to apply. To know more about channels, click [here](#). 

        <Note>Note: The feature of assigning channels to coupons is only available on the business plan subscription.</Note>
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_7.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Discount Details

Enter the **_discount value_** and set the **_minimum purchase amount_** on which the coupon applies.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_8.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Coupon Scope

Choose the scope of the coupon. You need to decide whether the coupon applies to **_specific categories_**, **_specific products_**, or the customer's **_entire order_**. In this guide, we will create a coupon for the Entire Order.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_9.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Coupon Usage

You can enable the toggle and set the total **_number of times_** the coupon can be used by your customers.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_10.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Coupon Duration

Choose the coupon's duration. Click on **_Valid From_** to select the start date and **_Valid To_** to select the end date.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_11.png" />
                    </Frame>

Upon clicking Valid From or Valid To, a calendar opens up where you can select your start and end dates.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_12.png" />
                    </Frame>

### Save Coupon

Click on **_Save_** to save your coupon.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_13.png" />
                    </Frame>

Your coupon has been successfully saved.
        <Frame>
                    <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/coupons/coupons_14.png" />
                    </Frame>
