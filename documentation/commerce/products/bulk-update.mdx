## Bulk Update

*How to Bulk Update Your Products*

*How to Update Several Products in Bulk*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

**STEP 1:**
Log in to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Products.

**STEP 2:**
Click on More Actions > Import.

**STEP 3:**
Click on Update Products.

**STEP 4:**
Select the channel whose products you wish to update.
*Note: The feature of choosing the channel whose products you wish to update is present only on the Business plan.*

**STEP 5:**
Click on the dropdown shown to select the category whose products you wish to update.

**STEP 6:**
Select your desired category. Here we will select the category Ready to Eat.

**STEP 7:**
Then choose the products you wish to update, and click on Download to download the update spreadsheet.

**STEP 8:**
The bulk update spreadsheet looks similar to the bulk upload sheet, with one key difference: Each category, product, variant, and brand has a unique ID associated with it. Since we need to edit a product, we will click on the Products tab shown below.

**STEP 9:**
We will be editing the VAT tax rate.
*Important note: The Tax Fields and Warehouses columns only appear in the bulk update sheet.*

**STEP 10:**
Once the sheet has been updated, go back to your aasaan admin panel, click on the Select a file to upload button and upload the bulk update sheet.

**STEP 11:**
Click on Upload.

**STEP 12:**
The update spreadsheet has been uploaded. As shown, the status says "In Progress". Click on the Refresh button.
Once the update process has finished, the status should say "Complete".
