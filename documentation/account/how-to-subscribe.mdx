### Goto your aasaan admin panel.
- Click on "Manage".
      <Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_1.png" />
               </Frame>

### Navigate to Account
- Click on "Account".
      <Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_2.png" />
               </Frame>

### Access Subscriptions
- Click on "Subscriptions".
      <Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_3.png" />
               </Frame>

### Choose a Plan
- View the available plans.
- Select the desired plan and duration (e.g., premium plan for one month).
      <Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_4.png" />
               </Frame>

### Payment Gateway
- Upon selecting a plan, the payment gateway opens.
- Enter your mobile number and email address.
- Click on "Proceed".
      <Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_5.png" />
               </Frame>

### Payment Process
- Choose your preferred payment method.
- Complete the payment process.
      <Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_6.png" />
               </Frame>

### Confirmation
- After successful payment, the subscription page should update with the details of the current plan.
      <Frame>
               <img src="https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_7.jpg" />
               </Frame>
