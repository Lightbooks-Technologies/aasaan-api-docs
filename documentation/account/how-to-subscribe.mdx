1. **Login:**
   - Access your aasaan admin panel.
   - Click on "Manage."
![Step 1](https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_1.png)

2. **Navigate to Account:**
   - Click on "Account."
![Step 2](https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_2.png)

3. **Access Subscriptions:**
   - Click on "Subscriptions."
![Step 3](https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_3.png)

4. **Choose a Plan:**
   - View the available plans.
   - Select the desired plan and duration (e.g., premium plan for one month).
![Step 4](https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_4.png)

5. **Payment Gateway:**
   - Upon selecting a plan, the payment gateway opens.
   - Enter your mobile number and email address.
   - Click on "Proceed."
![Step 5](https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_5.png)

6. **Payment Process:**
   - Choose your preferred payment method.
   - Complete the payment process.
![Step 6](https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_6.png)

7. **Confirmation:**
   - After successful payment, the subscription page should update with the details of the current plan.
![Step 7](https://cdn.aasaan.app/docs/how/how_7.jpg)
