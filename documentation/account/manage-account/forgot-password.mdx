## Forgot Password

**How to reset your password**  
*Reset your Password*

*Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

### STEP 1:

Go to your aasaan admin panel login page and click on Forgot your Password.

### STEP 2:

Enter your email address and click on Reset Password.

*Note: Merchants based in India can also use their registered mobile number for this purpose.*

### STEP 3:

Enter the authentication code sent to your email address. Then choose and reenter your new password and click on Submit.

You have successfully reset your password. You will be redirected to your aasaan admin panel login page.
