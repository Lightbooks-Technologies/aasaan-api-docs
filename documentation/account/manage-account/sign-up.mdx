## Sign Up

**How to Create your Store on aasaan**  
*Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

### STEP 1:

Visit our desktop admin panel on your browser: [https://admin.aasaan.app/](https://admin.aasaan.app/)

### STEP 2:

Click on Create yours now.

### STEP 3:

Now you can sign up by entering your email address, or by signing up with Google or Facebook.

Here we will sign up through email. Enter your email address and click on Continue.

Note: If you choose to sign up via Google or Facebook, you will be taken to step 5.

### STEP 4:

Enter the OTP sent to the email address, enter your name and confirm your password. Then click on Submit.

### STEP 5:

Enter your mobile number and click on Continue.

### STEP 6:

Enter the OTP sent to your number and click on Continue.

Note: Steps 5 and 6 only apply to stores based in India.

### STEP 7:

You will be taken to aasaan's welcome page. Click on Continue to proceed with your store creation.

### STEP 8:

Choose your store type and click on Continue. Here we will choose Others.

Note: You can choose your business category based on the kind of business you operate.

### STEP 9:

Enter your store name and click on Create Store. You will notice that your store country has been automatically assigned based on your location.

Click on Setup your Store.

You will see your store homepage. You have now successfully created your store on aasaan.

Note: When your store is created, it comes with some demo data based on the business category you chose in step 8. Click on the button "Clear Demo Data" to clear our demo data.
