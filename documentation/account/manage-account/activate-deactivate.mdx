## Activate/Deactivate

**How to deactivate and reactivate your store**  
*Deactivate & Reactivate your aasaan Store*

*Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

### How to Deactivate your Store

**STEP 1:**  
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Manage.

**STEP 2:**  
Click on Account.

**STEP 3:**  
Click on Deactivate Store.

**STEP 4:**  
When asked whether you wish to deactivate your store, click on Continue.

**STEP 5:**  
Choose the reason for store deactivation and click on Continue.

**STEP 6:**  
You will receive an authentication code. Choose whether you wish to receive it on your email address or mobile number (for Indian stores). Here we will select the mobile number and click on Continue.

**STEP 7:**  
Enter the authentication code and click on Deactivate Now.

**STEP 8:**  
Your store has been successfully deactivated. Click on Done. If you wish to, you can reactivate your store up to 2 months after it has been deactivated.

*Deactivated stores are marked as Inactive.*

### How to Reactivate your Store

**STEP 9:**  
Click on the store you wish to reactivate and confirm your request.

Your store has been successfully reactivated.
