## Registered Email Address

**How to change your registered email address in aasaan.**  
*Change your Registered Email Address*

*Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

### STEP 1:

Login to your aasaan admin panel, click on the settings icon in the top right corner, and then click on Profile Settings.

### STEP 2:

Click on Change Email.

### STEP 3:

Enter your new email address and click on Submit.

### STEP 4:

Enter the authentication code sent to the new email address and click on Submit.

You have successfully changed your registered email address.
