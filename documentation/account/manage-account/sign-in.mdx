## Sign In

**How to sign in to your aasaan admin panel**  
*How to Sign in to aasaan*

*Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

### STEP 1:

Go to your aasaan admin panel.

### STEP 2:

Here, you can log in to your store based on your signup method. For example, if you created your store via email, you can enter your email address and password.

Note: Store owners in India can also use their registered mobile number to login.

You can also request an authentication code to your email address and enter that to sign in.

Click on Login to submit your authentication code.

If you created your store via Google or Facebook, you need to only click on Continue with Google or Continue with Facebook respectively.
