## Logout

**How to logout from your admin panel.**  
*Logout From your aasaan Admin Panel*

*Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video-link).

### STEP 1:

In your aasaan admin panel, click on Manage.

### STEP 2:

Click on Account.

### STEP 3:

Click on Log Out.

You will then be redirected to your login page.
