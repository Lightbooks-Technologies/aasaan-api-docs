## Registered Mobile Number

**How to change your registered mobile number in aasaan.**  
*Changed your Registered Mobile Number*

*Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

### STEP 1:

Login to your aasaan admin panel, click on the settings icon in the top right corner, and then click on Profile Settings.

### STEP 2:

Click on Change Phone.

### STEP 3:

Enter your new mobile number and click on Submit.

### STEP 4:

Enter the authentication code sent to the new mobile number and click on Submit.

You have successfully changed your registered mobile number.
