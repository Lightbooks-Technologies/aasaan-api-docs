## Domain

### How to connect your domain

When you create your store on aasaan, by default, you get a mystore.aasaan.app URL. Upon subscription to a premium or higher plan, you can choose to connect your custom domain. To connect your domain to your store, you will first need to purchase a domain from a third-party provider such as GoDaddy or Hostinger. Connecting a custom domain to your store helps you establish your brand and build trust in the eyes of your customers.

---

In the articles in this section, you will learn how to connect your custom domain purchased from various providers.
