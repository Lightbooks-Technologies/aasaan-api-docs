## FOMO

*How to Add FOMOs to Your Products*
*Enable FOMOs on your Store*

Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.
You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

Here's how you can add FOMOs such as "Sold Out", "New", "Sale" and more to your products.

**STEP 1:**
Log into the desktop version of your aasaan app and click on Website Settings.

**STEP 2:**
Click on the settings icon.

**STEP 3:**
Click on FOMO - Fear of Missing Out.

**STEP 4:**
Enable the Sold Out toggle to add tags to the products that are sold out.
Enable the On Sale toggle to add tags to the products that are on sale.
Enable the New Arrivals toggle to add tags to the products that have been added in the past two days or more recently.
Enable the Quantity toggle to add tags to the products for which the quantity is less than 15.

You have successfully enabled FOMOs for your products.
