## Logo

*How to add your Store Logo*

Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.
You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

You can add your business logo to the aasaan app in just a few simple steps.

**STEP 1:**
Log in to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Manage.

**STEP 2:**
Click on Account.

**STEP 3:**
To add your logo, click on the logo icon and add your image. For best results, make sure your image aspect ratio is 3.5:1.

**STEP 4:**
Now it is time to adjust your logo image. Set the dimensions and position of the image using the drag and drop feature.

**STEP 6:**
Once you are satisfied with how your logo looks, click on Update.

**STEP 7:**
You have successfully added your store logo.
Your logo will be visible in the top left corner on your desktop website.
