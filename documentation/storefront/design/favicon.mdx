## Favicon

*Add your Favicon to your Website*
*Add your Store Favicon*

Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.
Note: You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

A favicon is the icon that you see on the tab of a desktop website.

Here's how to add a Favicon for your aasaan desktop website.

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Website Settings.

**STEP 2:**
Click on the settings icon.

**STEP 3:**
Click on Favicon.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Upload Favicon.

**STEP 5:**
Click on Save.

Note: For best results, make sure your image size is 192px * 192px.

Your favicon has been successfully uploaded.
