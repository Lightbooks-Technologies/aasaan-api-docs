## Floating Announcement Bar
### How to Add Floating Announcement Bar to your Website
#### Add Floating Announcement Bar at the Bottom of your Website

*With aasaan, you can add a floating announcement bar to the bottom of your website and have it say anything you like. For example, you might want to talk about free shipping all over India.*

**Here's how you do it:**

**STEP 1:**
Log in to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Storefront.

**STEP 2:**
Click on the content template in which you wish to add the floating announcement bar. You can also click on Create Template if you wish to create a new template for your website.

**STEP 3:**
Click on the + icon on the right as shown in the image below.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Static.

**STEP 5:**
Click on Announcement.

**STEP 6:**
Click on Floating Announcement Bar.

**STEP 7:**
Enter your desired text in the field provided.

**STEP 8:**
Choose a color for the announcement text from the color palette or enter the color code manually.

**STEP 9:**
Click on Styles.

**STEP 10:**
Choose a background color from the color palette or enter the color code manually.

**STEP 11:**
Click on Save.

**STEP 12:**
Click on Publish to publish your changes to your store.

*This is how your floating announcement bar will look on your website.*
