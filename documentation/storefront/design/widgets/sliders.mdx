## Sliders
### Display Sliders on your Website Homepage
#### Add Sliders to your Homepage

*Note: [Click here](mobile_app_link) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](video_link):

**STEP 1:**
Click on Website Settings.

**STEP 2:**
Choose a content template to add your New Arrivals widget in. 

*Note: If you wish to add it in a new template, click on Create a Template.*

**STEP 3:**
Click on the "+" sign on the right to create your sliders. 

**STEP 4:**
Click on Dynamic.

**STEP 5:**
Click on Fashion Sliders.

**STEP 6:**
Click on Add Image to upload the slider image for your mobile and desktop websites. 

**STEP 7:**
Enter the slider title and tagline.

**STEP 8:**
Choose the background color for your slider.

**STEP 9:**
Now you need to link your slider to a product or category. Here we will link it to a product. Click on Add Product.

*Notes:* 
1. Check the Show Action Button box if you wish to display the shop now button on your slider.

**STEP 10:**
Select the product you wish to link to your slider and click on Done.

*Note: You can click on Add Slider to add more sliders in your widget.*

**STEP 11:**
Click on Styles to configure the styling of your slider.

**STEP 12:**
In the Styles section, you can customize the style elements of your slider such as padding, the background color around the slider and borders. 

**STEP 13:**
After customizing your styles, click on Visibility.

**STEP 14:**
In the Visibility section, you can decide which devices (desktop, tablet, smartphone) on which you would like to display your slider. 

**STEP 15:**
After customizing your visibility settings, click on Save to save your slider.

**STEP 16:**
You have successfully saved your slider. You can see a preview of your slider. When satisfied with how it looks, click on the Publish button to publish your slider to your homepage.

Your slider has been published to your website.
