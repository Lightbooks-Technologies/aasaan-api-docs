## About
### Use the About static block to tell your business story
#### How to use the About Block

You can watch the video version of the article [here](video_link).

If you want to narrate your business and brand story in the best way, you can do that with our "About" block. The steps to creating your "About" block are as follows:

**STEP 1:**
Login to your Aasaan admin panel and click on Website Settings.

**STEP 2:**
Click on an existing content template in which you wish to add your page. If you wish, you can also create a new content template by clicking on +Create Template.

**STEP 3:**
Click on Pages.

**STEP 4:**
Click on About Us.

**STEP 5:**
Click on the "Plus" Sign to add a new block to your About Us page.

**STEP 6:**
Click on Static.

**STEP 7:**
Expand the About dropdown and choose your desired template.

**STEP 8:**
Upload your desired image by clicking on Add Image.

**STEP 9:**
Enter the heading and description.

**STEP 10:**
Click on the downward arrow to expand the list-1.

**STEP 11:**
Enter your list heading and description.

**STEP 12:**
Click on Add New List to add more list items.

**STEP 13:**
You can alter the styling and visibility settings of your About block by clicking on Styles and Visibility respectively.

**STEP 14:**
Once you are satisfied with your About block, click on Save.

**STEP 15:**
You have successfully saved your About block. Click on Publish to make it go live on your website. You have successfully published your About block to your website.
