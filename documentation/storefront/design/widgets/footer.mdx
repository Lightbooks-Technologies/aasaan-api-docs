## Footer
### How to add Footer to your Website.
#### Add footer to your website

*With aasaan, you can now add a footer to your website. A website footer is a designated section located at the bottom of a web page, containing essential and consistent information such as contact details, links to privacy policies, and other relevant links.*

**Here's how you do it:**

**STEP 1:**
Log in to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Storefront.

**STEP 2:**
Click on the content template in which you wish to add the footer. You can also click on Create Template if you wish to create a new template for your website.

**STEP 3:**
Click on the + icon on the right as shown in the image below.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Static.

**STEP 5:**
Click on Footer.

**STEP 6:**
Select your desired footer template from the list given. Here we will choose Footer Demo.

**STEP 7:**
The Footer Demo template allows you to add your exact location on google maps to your website. To do so, enter your desired google map link in the field provided.

*Note: If you prefer not to display the phone number, email address, and address details in the footer, you can simply deselect the corresponding check boxes.*

**STEP 8:**
Click on Styles.

**STEP 9:**
Click on Background Color to add the background color for the footer.
Choose the background color for the footer from the color palette or enter the color code manually.

**STEP 10:**
Click on Save.

**STEP 11:**
Click on Publish to publish your changes to your store.

*Once published, this is how your demo footer will look on your website.*
