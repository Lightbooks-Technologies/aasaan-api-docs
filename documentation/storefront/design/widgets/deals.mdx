## Deals
### Display Deals on your Website Homepage
#### Add Deals to your Homepage
*Note: [Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article](mobile_admin_app_link)*

*You can watch the video version of this article [here](video_link)*

**STEP 1:**
Click on Website Settings.

**STEP 2:**
Click on the content template you wish to add your deals widget in.
*Note: You can also create a new template by clicking on Create a Content Template.*

**STEP 3:**
Click on the "Plus" sign to add your deals widget.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Dynamic.

**STEP 5:**
Expand the deals dropdown and click on Basic Deals.

**STEP 6:**
Click on the start and end dates to choose your deal duration.
*When you click on a date, a calendar opens up with which you can set your start and end dates.*

**STEP 7:**
Click on Add Product to link one or more products to your deal.

**STEP 8:**
Choose the products you wish to display in your deal and click on Done.

**STEP 9:**
After setting your deal duration and choosing your products, click on Styles to configure the styling for your deals widget.

**STEP 10:**
You can customize various style elements such as padding, background color, and borders.
*Once you have configured your styles, click on Visibility to manage the visibility settings across different kinds of devices for your Deal.*

**STEP 11:**
Set the visibility of your New Arrivals for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
*Click on Settings to add the title for your New Arrivals.*

**STEP 12:**
Enter the deals widget title and click on Save.
Your Deals widget has been saved. You can preview the widget in your admin panel as shown.

**STEP 13:**
Click on Publish to push your deal to the website homepage.
You have successfully published your deals widget to your website.
