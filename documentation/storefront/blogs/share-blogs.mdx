## Share Blogs

*How to Share Your Blog Articles*
*Share Blog Articles on your Website*

Here is how you can share your blog articles from your desktop admin panel.

**STEP 1:**
Go to your blogs page and click on the share button for the article you wish to share.

**STEP 2:**
Click on the corresponding icon to share your article on Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter or Whatsapp.
