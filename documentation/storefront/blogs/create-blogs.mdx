## Create Blogs

*How to Add Blogs to your Website*
*Create Blogs on your Store*

[Note: Click here to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article](#)

[You can watch the video version of this article here](#)

Here is how you can add blogs to your aasaan website:

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Blog on the left hand side.

**STEP 2:**
First, we will create a category which will contain the blog article. Click on New in the categories section.

**STEP 3:**
Enter the category name, description and click on Save.

**STEP 4:**
Next, we will add an author. Click on Author.

**STEP 5:**
Click on New to add an author.

**STEP 6:**
Enter the author's name and designation and upload their image. Then click on Save.

**STEP 7:**
Now that the author has been created, you can start writing your actual blog article. Click on Blog.

**STEP 8:**
To start creating your article, click on New.

**STEP 9:**
Enter the blog name, description, categories and author.

**STEP 10:**
Click on Add Image to upload the featured and cinematic images.

**STEP 11:**
Write your article in the field shown and click on Save.
Your blog article has been successfully saved.
You can view your blogs on your storefront website by going to the footer and clicking on Blog.
Click on the thumbnail of the article you wish to read.
Your article is now visible.
