## Perform SEO for your Aasaan website homepage

### How to Perform Homepage SEO for your Website

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

**STEP 1:**
Log in to your Aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Store Front.

**STEP 2:**
Click on the settings icon.

**STEP 3:**
Click on SEO.

**STEP 4:**
Add the SEO image, Page title, and Page meta description.
*Note: The preview image will be visible whenever you share your website URL on social media.*
Click on Save.

You have successfully set up the SEO for your website homepage.
