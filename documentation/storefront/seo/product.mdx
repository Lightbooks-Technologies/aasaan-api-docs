## How to optimize your product pages for SEO

### How to Perform Product SEO for your Website

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

In the previous article, we covered how to perform Search Engine Optimization for your website homepage. In this article, you will learn how to perform SEO for your product pages as well.

**STEP 1:**
Login to your Aasaan desktop admin panel and go to the product you wish to perform SEO on.

**STEP 2:**
Scroll down and enable the toggle for the SEO section.

**STEP 3:**
Add the Preview image, Page title, and Page meta description and click on Save to save the product.
