## Social Media

### How to Add your Social Media Links to your Website

#### Display your Social Media Pages on your Store

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article.*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

If you have pages on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest, you can link them on your website's footer. Here's how.

**STEP 1:**

Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Store Front.

**STEP 2:**

Click on the settings icon.

**STEP 3:**

Click on Social.

**STEP 4:**

Click on any drop-down arrow to link the corresponding social media page. For example, here we will click on the drop-down for Facebook, to add our Facebook page.

**STEP 5:**

Enter the page URL. Click on Save.

**STEP 6:**

Your Facebook page has been successfully added.

You can similarly add links to your Instagram page, Twitter page, and Pinterest page.

Your website visitors can go to your social media pages by clicking on the respective icons in your website's footer.
