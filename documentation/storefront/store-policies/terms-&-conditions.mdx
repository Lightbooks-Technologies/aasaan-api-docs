## Terms & Conditions

### Set up your Store's Terms & Conditions

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Store Front.

**STEP 2:**
Click on an existing content template in which you wish to add your page. If you wish, you can also create a new content template by clicking on +Create Template. *Note: If you are subscribed to the business plan, you can choose which channel you wish to add your content template and custom page in as shown below. [Click here](#) to learn more about channels.*

**STEP 3:**
Click on Pages.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Terms & Conditions. This will take you to the Terms & Conditions page.

**STEP 5:**
Click on the "Plus" Sign to add a new block to your Terms & Conditions page.

**STEP 6:**
Click on Static.

**STEP 7:**
Expand the Policy section and click on Policy 1.

**STEP 8:**
Here you can either add your own Terms & Conditions, or use our template. If you wish to use our template, click on Select Template.

**STEP 9:**
Click on Terms & Condition Details.

**STEP 10:**
This is how the template looks. You can use this as your Terms & Conditions, or you can modify it to suit your requirements.

**STEP 11:**
You can customize the styling and visibility settings of your Terms & Conditions by clicking on Styles and Visibility respectively.

**STEP 12:**
Once you are satisfied, click on Save.

**STEP 13:**
Your Terms & Conditions has been successfully saved. Click on Publish to make your policy live on your website. Your Terms & Conditions has been successfully published to your website.
