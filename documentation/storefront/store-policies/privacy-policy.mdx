## Privacy Policy

### Add Privacy Policy To Your Website

### Set up your Store's Privacy Policy

*Note: Click [here](#) to navigate to the mobile admin app version of this article*

You can watch the video version of this article [here](#).

**STEP 1:**
Login to your aasaan desktop admin panel and click on Website Settings.

**STEP 2:**
Click on an existing content template in which you wish to add your page. If you wish, you can also create a new content template by clicking on +Create Template. *Note: If you are subscribed to the business plan, you can choose which channel you wish to add your content template and custom page in as shown below. [Click here](#) to learn more about channels.*

**STEP 3:**
Click on Pages.

**STEP 4:**
Click on Privacy Policy. This will take you to the privacy policy page.

**STEP 5:**
Click on the "Plus" Sign to add a new block to your Privacy Policy page.

**STEP 6:**
Click on Static.

**STEP 7:**
Expand the Policy section and click on Policy 1.

**STEP 8:**
Here you can either add your own privacy policy, or use our template. If you wish to use our template, click on Select Template.

**STEP 9:**
Click on Privacy Policy Details.

**STEP 10:**
A sample privacy policy has been generated. You can modify it as per your store.

**STEP 11:**
You can customize the styling and visibility settings of your privacy policy by clicking on Styles and Visibility respectively.

**STEP 12:**
Once you are satisfied with your privacy policy, click on Save.

**STEP 13:**
Your privacy policy has been successfully saved. Click on Publish to make your policy live on your website. Your privacy policy has been successfully published to your website.
